Food Delivery System Data Analysis
This repository contains a Jupyter Notebook (food_delivery_analysis.ipynb) that provides a comprehensive analysis of food delivery data. The analysis explores key performance indicators, customer behavior, and operational metrics to derive actionable insights.

ðŸ“ˆ Analysis Overview
The notebook is structured to analyze several key facets of the food delivery business:

Revenue Analysis:

Calculates total revenue and average order value.

Visualizes daily revenue trends over time.

Analyzes average revenue by the day of the week.

Identifies the top 10 restaurants by total revenue.

Delivery Performance:

Calculates mean, median, and standard deviation for delivery times.

Determines the on-time delivery rate (using a 45-minute threshold).

Visualizes the distribution of delivery times with a histogram.

Customer Satisfaction:

Analyzes the distribution of customer ratings (1-5 stars).

Calculates the overall average customer rating.

Visualizes the rating distribution with a bar chart.

Payment Methods:

Visualizes the distribution of different payment methods (e.g., Credit Card, PayPal, Cash) using a pie chart.

ðŸ’¡ Key Insights
The analysis in the notebook uncovers the following key insights:

Total Revenue: The total revenue generated over the analysis period.

Average Delivery Time: The mean time taken for an order to be delivered.

Average Customer Rating: The overall average customer rating on a 5-star scale.

Most Popular Payment Method: The payment method most frequently used by customers.

Top City by Orders: The city with the highest volume of orders.

Top Restaurant by Revenue: The restaurant that generated the most revenue.
